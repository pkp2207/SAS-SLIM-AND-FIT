About Us & Our Founder

SAS Fat To Slim is a pioneering Indian-based diet and nutrition service, dedicated to crafting personalized nutrition plans rooted in traditional Indian home cooking. With a track record of over 30,000 satisfied clients across India and worldwide, we stand as a trusted authority in the realm of nutrition and weight loss.

At SAS Fat To Slim, our founder, Shikha Aggarwal Sharma, leads with a distinct philosophy. Unlike conventional approaches that prescribe expensive short-term diets or advocate starvation, Shikha emphasizes enhancing metabolism and digestion through the utilization of commonly available kitchen spices and herbs. Her approach ensures that our diets are not only effective but also affordable and comfortable for all.
